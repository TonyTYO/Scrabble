Welcome to Scrabble

This is a normal scrabble game for two players using the SOWPODS dictionary. All the usual rules apply.

The current player moves tiles from the rack onto the board with the mouse or finger and drops them on a square.

They will remain moveable until the End turn button is pressed to end the turn. The other player's rack will be dimmed.


The following buttons are available while playing.

Start turn: Press to start your turn.
You will not be able do anything before pressing this.		 End turn: Press to mark the end of your turn.
You will not be able to move any tiles after pressing this.
Pass: Press to pass on the turn. Exchange: Press to exchange tiles.
Challenge: Activated for the other player after End has been pressed. Press within 5 secs to challenge the words. Leave: Press this to go to the scorecard or end the game.

The following buttons are available after pressing Leave.

Return: Press to return to the game. New: Press to start a new game.
Quit: Press to quit.
